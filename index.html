RATAN TATA

28 December 1937 - 9 October 2024

A visionary leader and philanthropist

Ratan Tata is an Indian industrialist, philanthropist, and former chairman of Tata Sons. He has led the Tata Group, India's largest multinational conglomerate, to new heights and is known for his humble, ethical, and people-centric approach.

Notable Achievements

  • Chairman of Tata Group from 1991 to 2012, and again from 2016 to 2017.
  • Launched the Tata Nano, the world's cheapest car.
  • Acquisition of Jaguar Land Rover and Corus Steel under his leadership.
  • Significant philanthropic contributions through Tata Trusts in health, education, and rural development.

Quotes

"I don’t believe in taking right decisions. I take decisions and then make them right."

— Ratan Tata

Legacy

Ratan Tata's contribution to business and society goes beyond just profit margins. His vision for a better India, combined with a strong sense of ethics and responsibility, makes him a true leader of the modern world.
